# Welcome to Kelime Türetme Oyunu

Kelime Türetme Oyunu is a new word finding game using the last letter of the word.

## Rules

- You must derive a new word that starts with the last letter of the word
- You can't repeat the words that came out before
- Within eight seconds, you must say the word you derived
- When it's your turn, just say it (microphone pops up to listen to your voice)
- You may need to grant microphone permission to play
